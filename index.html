Spatial Analysis of Factors Associated With Sexual Violence in Kingston, Ontario

The spatial distribution of sexual offences in Kingston, Ontario was examined relative to potential associated factors. I determined whether existing sexual violence resources are located optimally.

Preventative Healthcare Site Selection Model Builder

In collaboration with my Lab partner, Jess Pearson, we created a model builder that takes municipal data and selects potential sites for preventative healthcare. This is specifically geared towards increasing access for Indigenous reserves.

Read More

Preventative Healthcare Site Selection Script Tool (Python)

This script tool is based on the earlier model builder. It selects potential sites for preventative healthcare centers while considering several factors such as access from Indigenous reserves. This is based on research showing that reserves lack access to preventative healthcare.
